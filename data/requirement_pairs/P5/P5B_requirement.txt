The system shall allow a student to reserve a library study room for a selected date and time slot. The student shall choose the room size, enter the number of participants, provide a contact email address, and optionally request a projector. If a projector is requested, the system shall check both room availability and projector availability before creating the reservation. If the room is available but the projector is unavailable, the system shall offer the student the option to reserve the room without the projector or choose a different time slot. The confirmation message shall describe which resources were reserved.
