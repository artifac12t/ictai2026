The system shall allow an employee to submit an IT support ticket by entering a problem description, selecting a device type, and attaching optional screenshots. If the employee marks the ticket as involving account access, suspicious email, or possible data exposure, the system shall route the ticket to the security team instead of assigning it directly to an available support technician. The system shall notify the employee only after the security team completes the review or transfers the ticket back to general support. The system shall store both the ticket history and the security review record for future reference.
