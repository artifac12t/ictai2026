The system shall allow a customer to place an online order by selecting products, entering a shipping address, and choosing a saved payment method. After the customer submits the order, the system shall create a pending order record and reserve the selected products while inventory availability is checked. The system shall charge the saved payment method only after all selected products are confirmed to be available. If one or more products are unavailable, the system shall ask the customer to revise the order before payment is charged. The final order receipt shall be sent only after payment is completed.
