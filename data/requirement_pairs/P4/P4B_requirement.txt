The system shall allow a student team to submit one assignment before the deadline by uploading one shared file and adding an optional comment for the instructor. After submission, the system shall store the file as the team submission and link the submission record to every team member. After grading is completed, the system shall show the team grade and feedback to each team member unless the instructor assigns an individual override grade to a specific member. Late submissions shall be marked as late for the team submission record, not separately for each member.
